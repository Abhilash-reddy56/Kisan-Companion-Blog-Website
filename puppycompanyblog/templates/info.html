Kisan-Companion


Kisan-Companion lets you know the recommendation's of Crop, Fertilizer and Disease-Detection.
This a website in which farmers can post their progress about the crops or the status about the fertilizers they are using.
This even helps other farmers to communicate among themselves and articulate each other

CROP

To get the recommendation of crop you need to provide some of the soil details like

  • Nitrogen - N
  • Phosphorous - P
  • Potassium - K
  • ph level of soil - ph
  • Rainfall in mm
  • State
  • City
And that's it you will get the crop to be grown based on our Machine Learning Model

FERTILIZER

To get the recommendation of fertilizer you need to provide some of the soil details like

  • Nitrogen - N
  • Phosphorous - P
  • Potassium - K
  • Recommened Crop from the previous section
And that's it you will get the fertilizer to be used based on our Machine Learning Model

DISEASE - DETECTION

To get the cure and precaution about the disease of the plant. You need to just upload the leaf photo. And that's it you will get the complete details about the disease based on our Machine Learning Model.

It even suggests the precautions of the to be followed in the further process.
